Risk Factors

HIGH RISK
Competition from Big Tech
OpenAI, Google, or Microsoft could launch competing agent registry with massive resources. Mitigation: Focus on open-source community trust, specialized vertical recipes.
HIGH RISK
LLM Cost Volatility
Guardian agent costs could spike if LLM pricing increases or usage exceeds projections. Mitigation: Budget 2x current estimates, implement usage caps, explore self-hosted LLM options.
MEDIUM RISK
Slow Organic Growth
User acquisition may be slower than projected without significant marketing investment. Mitigation: Strong developer relations, conference presence, integration partnerships.
MEDIUM RISK
Recipe Quality Control
Maintaining recipe quality and preventing malicious submissions as volume scales. Mitigation: Multi-layered guardian agents, community moderation, reputation system.
LOW RISK
Infrastructure Scaling
Vector DB performance and cost optimization challenges as usage grows. Mitigation: Proven Qdrant scalability, incremental optimization, established AWS patterns.
